About the Developer

Originally from Jeffersonville, IN. Blake Camille grew up with a love of sports. In particular, he loved football and basketball. He also shared a passion for music, where later on in life, he began creating his own electronic compositions. He eventually graduated from Jeffersonville High School in 2006. The next year, he started his college studies in video and audio production at the Art Institute of Atlanta.

While attening AIA, Blake continued to create his own music, mostly in the genres of Hip Hop and R&B. Unfortunately, due to the expenses of attending the school. Blake left AIA and traveled back to his home state to attend IU Bloomington. He studied Telecommunications with a focus in film and tv production. He graduated IU in 2013 and gained even more of a passion for production.

Life after college has definitely been a journey for Blake. Doing jobs such as audio operations and technical directing for a local CBS station in Louisville, KY. Eventually, Blake moved back to Atlanta where he enjoyed the environment, community, and the music space that Atlanta provided. While in Atlanta, he worked for AT&T as a wire technician all the way up until 2020 when he and his wife, Andreana Camille, wanted to make a change of scenery for their family. At the end of 2020, Blake started the Oakland Police Academy and graduated the next year to become a sworn police officer. Since that time, Blake has been dabbling with the world of software development. Learning skills such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, he strives to become a valuable developer to a great team. Blake wants to contribute to the development in any way he can.
